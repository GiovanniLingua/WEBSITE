About Us

FBC Chemicals (Pty) Ltd (Reg. K2017471935) is a well-established business in Thabazimbi, Limpopo, providing wholesale and retail chemical solutions to the agricultural, wildlife, and related sectors.

We specialise in effective chemical products and knowledgeable advice for chemiese ontbossing (bush clearing), plaagbeheer (pest control), lyndraad produkte (fence line maintenance), and pasture improvement.

  • Chemical bush encroachment control & pasture reclamation
  • Weed killers and herbicides
  • Pesticides and pest control solutions
  • Fence line & boundary treatment products
  • Expert guidance for wildsboer (game farmers) and agriculture
  • Competitive wholesale & retail supply
