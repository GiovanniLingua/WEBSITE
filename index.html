What is the difference between managed IT services and break-fix services?

Managed IT services involve proactive, continuous support where a provider monitors and maintains your IT infrastructure for a fixed fee. They focus on preventing issues and offer a long-term partnership. In contrast, break-fix services are reactive, involving payment only when issues arise, without ongoing monitoring or commitment.

What is cloud computing, and how can it benefit my organization?

Cloud computing is a technology that enables organizations to access and utilize computing resources (like servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and more) over the internet on a pay-as-you-go basis. This approach offers several benefits, including cost-efficiency, scalability, flexibility, and the ability to access resources from anywhere with an internet connection. It can benefit your organization by reducing infrastructure costs, improving agility, enabling remote work, and allowing you to focus on core business activities while the cloud provider handles IT infrastructure management and maintenance.
